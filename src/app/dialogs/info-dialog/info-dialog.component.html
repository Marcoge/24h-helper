How to use

  • Add driver by filling in the name in the top left corner and press enter or click "Add" button.
  • Reorder driver list by dragging the driver item with the arrow icon.
  • Delete driver by dragging the driver item to the trash bin icon below the driver list.
  • Add Stint by clicking the floating button in the bottom right corner.
  • Drivers and stints will be saved in local storage. When you close and reopen the app you can pick up where you left.
  • Darkmode can be enabled/disabled with the toggle in the top right.
  • Info can be viewed again by clicking the info button in the top right.
