How to use

  • Add driver by filling in the name in the top left corner and press enter or click "Add" button.
  • Reorder driver list by dragging the driver item with the arrow icon.
  • Delete driver by dragging the driver item to the bin icon below the driver list.
  • Add Stint by clicking the floatin button in the bottom right corner.
  • Darkmode can be enabled/disabled with the toggle in the top right.
